## Submit Sheet Music for the Catalog

Have you purchased new sheet music for the Lionel Hampton School of Music library? We'd love to add it to our public catalog!

### How to Submit

1. **Fill out the submission form below** with details about the work
2. All submissions are reviewed by the music library staff
3. Approved entries are added to the catalog within 1-2 weeks
4. You'll receive a confirmation email when your submission is published

### What Information Do I Need?

- **Title of Work** (required) - The full title of the piece
- **Composer** (required) - Use "Last, First" format (e.g., "Gershwin, George")
- **Ensemble Type** (required) - What type of ensemble is it for?
- **Publisher** - Who published the work?
- **Envelope/Call Number** - Where will it be shelved in the library?
- **Number of Copies** - How many copies did you purchase?
- **Score Type** - Full score, condensed score, or oversized?
- **Playing Time** - Approximate duration (e.g., "3:45" or "approx. 4 min")
- **Missing Parts?** - Are any parts missing from the set?
- **Comments** - Any additional notes (condition, purchase date, etc.)

### Questions?

If you have questions about the submission process, contact the Music Library at [your-email@uidaho.edu](mailto:your-email@uidaho.edu).
